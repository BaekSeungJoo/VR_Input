Detailed Description

SnapInteractors can snap to poses provided by SnapInteractables. SnapInteractors select and unselect in response to a PointableElement. SnapInteractors hover when the PointableElement has selecting pointers and snap to the best SnapInteractable pose when the PointableElement has no more selecting pointers.

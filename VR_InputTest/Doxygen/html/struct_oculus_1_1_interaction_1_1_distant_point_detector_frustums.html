ConicalFrustum _aidFrustum
 When provided, items need to be also inside this frustum as well as the selection one to be selected. e.g. Selection frustum coming out from the hand, and aid frustum from the head so items need to be pointed at with the gaze and hand.
 
float _aidBlending
 Blends the scores between the Selection and Aid frustums. At 0 items centered in the selection frustum are given preference, at one items centered at the aid frustum are given preference.
